Order Of Omega - Xi Pi Chapter Initiation

Published on December 1st, 2016

"The National Order of Omega is an honor society for fraternity and sorority members. Members are selected based on character, scholarship, participation in the Greek community and service to the university and the at large Newark community. Students who have completed 60 credits or more, with a cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher are eligible to apply for membership. Membership in The Order is based on an application process and is limited to 3% of the entire Greek community." - NJIT
